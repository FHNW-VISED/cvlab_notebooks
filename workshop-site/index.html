About the Workshop

In this workshop we ask a deceptively simple question: how does AI actually "see" an image? Through two interactive Jupyter notebooks you'll experiment with pretrained CNNs (fast, strong results) and build your own CNN from scratch — comparing results, visualizing what the network focuses on with Grad-CAM, and developing an intuition for what's happening inside these models.


No deep learning background required. Bring curiosity and a laptop.
